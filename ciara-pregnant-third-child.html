    Congratulations are in order for Ciara and her husband Russell Wilson. The "Goodies" singer announced on Instagram that she’s expecting her third child.

    Ciara debuted the news today with an absolutely stunning photograph of herself sporting a bikini and showing off her burgeoning baby bump as she poses atop a hill during vacation in Turks and Caicos.

    “Number 3,” Ciara captioned the gorgeous shot. She also noted that proud dad Wilson took the image.

    The latest addition to the Wilson clan will join Ciara’s daughter, Sienna Princess Wilson, whom she welcomed with her NFL player hubby in 2017, and her five-year-old son Future Zahir Wilburn, who she shares with her ex Future.

    Wilson also celebrated the baby news on Instagram. The Seattle Seahawks player posted a beachside selfie of himself donning aviator sunglasses and sandy curls, while Ciara subtly strikes a pose in the background. Wilson also captioned his announcement simply stating, “Number 3." The couple have been married since July 2016.

    Ciara previously told BAZAAR.com that her and Wilson's relationship is "even greater… just… so… amazing… awesome… It really is." She later added, "If someone's not supporting you and your vision and your dreams, whether it's your relationship partner, your friend, whomever, then you've got to make sure to keep it moving."
