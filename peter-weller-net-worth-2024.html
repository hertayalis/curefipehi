His career spans over four decades, during which he has made significant contributions to the film and television industry.     Peter Weller’s Acting Career

    Weller’s acting career began in the 1970s, with his first significant role in the 1984 film “Firstborn”. However, it was his role as RoboCop in 1987 that catapulted him to international fame. The success of the film and its sequel significantly boosted his net worth.

    Notable Film Roles

    • “RoboCop” (1987)
    • “RoboCop 2” (1990)
    • “Naked Lunch” (1991)
    • “Star Trek Into Darkness” (2013)

    Television Roles

    Weller has also had a successful career in television, appearing in popular series such as “24”, “Dexter”, “Sons of Anarchy”, and “The Last Ship”. His television roles have contributed significantly to his net worth.

    Directing Career

    Aside from acting, Weller has also made a name for himself as a director. He has directed episodes for several television series, including “Hawaii Five-0”, “Sons of Anarchy”, and “House”. His directing career has added another stream of income to his net worth.

    Academic Career

    In addition to his acting and directing career, Weller is also a history lecturer. He earned a master’s degree in Roman and Renaissance Art in 2004 and a Ph.D. in Italian Renaissance Art History in 2014. He has taught at Syracuse University and UCLA, further diversifying his income sources.

    Real Estate Investments

    Weller has made several real estate investments over the years. He owns properties in Los Angeles and Santa Fe, which have significantly appreciated in value, contributing to his net worth.

    Endorsements and Sponsorships

    Like many celebrities, Weller has also earned income from endorsements and sponsorships. He has endorsed brands like Prada and has appeared in commercials for various products.

    Charitable Works

    Weller is also known for his charitable works. He is a board member of the Ilya Piatetski-Shapiro Foundation, which supports mathematical research. While his charitable works may not directly contribute to his net worth, they certainly add to his reputation and public image.

    Future Projects

    As of 2024, Weller is set to appear in several upcoming projects, including the film “The Last Full Measure”. These projects are expected to further increase his net worth.

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, Peter Weller’s net worth in 2024 is estimated to be around $10 million. This wealth has been accumulated from his successful acting and directing career, his academic pursuits, real estate investments, endorsements, and sponsorships. With several upcoming projects in the pipeline, his net worth is expected to continue growing. Despite his success, Weller remains committed to his academic and charitable pursuits, demonstrating that he is not just a talented actor and director, but also a dedicated scholar and philanthropist.
