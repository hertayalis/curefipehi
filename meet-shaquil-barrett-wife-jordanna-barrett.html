    Shaq Barrett is a professional American football player who currently plays as an outside linebacker for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers in the National Football League (NFL). He was born on November 17, 1992, in Baltimore, Maryland.

    Barrett played college football at Colorado State University and was signed as an undrafted free agent by the Denver Broncos in 2014. He spent his first four seasons with the Broncos, primarily as a backup linebacker and special teams player, before signing with the Buccaneers in 2019.

    In his first season with the Buccaneers, Barrett had a breakout year, recording 19.5 sacks, which led the NFL and set a new franchise record. He also earned his first Pro Bowl selection and was named a first-team All-Pro.
