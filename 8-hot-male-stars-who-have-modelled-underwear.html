Showing off his extensive tattoo collection and muscular physique, the 20-year-old singer’s racy new ads immediately drew comparisons to Mark Wahlberg’s infamous CK underwear campaign back in the 1990s.

    “Of course I’ve seen the Mark Wahlberg/Kate Moss ads,” said Justin. “They are over 20 years old and we’re still talking about them today – I was definitely inspired by them.”
