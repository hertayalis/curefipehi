    "KUWTK" star Kourtney Kardashian promoted "Poosh's" Matcha - ground green tea powder - with her most recent Instagram Post. Kardashian, 41, wears a tight black crop top and tight shorts showing off her toned body.

    The mom of three sits with her right knee bent, while her left leg is crossed over towards the front, as she grabs her right hand over her left shoulder, pulling it in a stretch. The star captioned the post with all the health benefits found in the tea beverage.

    Kourtney Kardashian spotted in New York City sipping on her favorite green drink, Match. October, 2020. | Photo: Getty Images.

    Her followers were full of compliments, with one commenter writing she is "obsessed" with Kardashian, while a few others called her "stunning" and "gorgeous." One commenter asked under Kardashian's caption:

    "Kourtney, how do you always look so good?"

    It must have something to do with the tea's immune-boosting benefits, which Kardashian often speaks about. The star has said it has anti-aging properties, targets inflammation in the body, and speeds up the body's energy-burning process.

    The "Poosh" founder shared a Matcha recipe through the business' website as part of a tasty twist to sweetening her favorite green drink, which Kourtney has said she enjoys either hot or cold.

    Barker has had a crush on the reality TV star for a long time.

    "Poosh" editor Michelle Scanga noted that while most people add just milk to their green latte, Kardashian gave hers an extra kick by including organic maple syrup.

    Kardashian went Instagram official this week by posting a snap of her hand being held by "Blink 182's," Travis Barker, 45. Barker shared the same picture on his Instagram story. Kardashian's friend, Khadijah Haqq McCray, commented on the post:

    "When friends become lovers."

    The two reportedly spent Valentine's Day together, with both posting a picture of a cozy fireplace on Sunday evening on their Instagram stories. A source confirmed the relationship to "People" last month.

    Fans of Kardashian witnessed her relationship with Scott Disick on the reality TV show, and the pair welcomed a son Mason, 11, and two daughters Reign, 6, and Penelope, 8.

    Meanwhile, Barker was married to Shanna Moakler from 2004 to 2008, and they shared two girls, Atiana, 21, Alabama, 15, and a son, Landon, 17. Atiana is Barker's stepdaughter.

    According to insiders close to the Kardashians, Barker has had a crush on the reality TV star for a long time, and the two have been friends for quite some time. The source added that Kardashian recently opened up to the idea of a relationship.

    The insider added that Barker is a hit with Kardashian's children, as well as the rest of her clan. The musician is credited for being a decent guy who gets along well with everyone.
