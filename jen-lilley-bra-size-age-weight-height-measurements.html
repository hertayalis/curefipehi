    Biography:

    Jen Lilley was born on August 4, 1984 in Roanoke, Virginia, United States. After graduating from high school in 2003, she attended the University of Virginia where she graduated Magna Cum Laude. Her professional acting career started in 2007, appearing in an episode of Hannah Montana. She has since appeared as a guest star in Criminal Minds and Castle.

    Her first major role was as Maxie Jones in the long-running soap opera General Hospital, playing the part from 2011 to 2012. She was next cast for the role of Theresa Donovan in the soap opera Days of Our Lives. She has also appeared in the movies Changeling and The Artist, playing minor characters.
