    Larry The Cable Guy Bio & Real Name

    Larry The Cable Guy was born Daniel Lawrence Whitney on February 17, 1963 in Pawnee City, Nebraska. In Nebraska, he was raised on a pig farm by his parents.

    He attended King’s Academy in Florida for his secondary education; his father was the primary school principal at King’s Academy. In 1982 he began attending Berean Christian School in West Palm Beach.

    He attended the University of Nebraska for his tertiary education. At the University of Nebraska, he majored in theater and speech. But as fate would have it, he withdrew from college after trying acting. His roommates, originally from Georgia and Texas, have influenced the southern accent with which he performs.

    His wife, sister and family

    His mother was Shirley Whitney and his father was Tom Whitney, a Christian minister, artist, and guitarist. He has a sister, Debbie Whitney and a brother, Tom Whitney Jr.

    In 2005, he married his wife, Cara Whitney on July 3. The couple now have two children – a son, Wyatt Whitney, was born on August 2, 2006. Their daughter was born on October 29, 2007.

    Back in his hometown of Pawnee City, a street was named for him.
