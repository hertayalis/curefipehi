    Kyle Richards and Mauricio Umansky, the longstanding couple featured on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, have just announced their split after 27 years of marriage. Richards became part of the popular Bravo reality series in 2010, with her husband Umansky making regular appearances alongside her over the last 13 years. The future of Real Housewives has become a hot topic as the series prepares for its thirteenth season following a gruesome Season 12.

    Recommended Videos

    Many reports of the show have been stirring up its cast, and many popular housewives, including Lisa Rinna and Diana Jenkins, are announcing their exits from the show after the intense three-part reunion that aired back in late 2022. But rest assured, Kyle Richards, along with Erika Jayne, Garcelle Beauvais, Dorit Kemsley, Crystal Kung Minkoff, and Sutton Stracke, will return to Real Housewives and make Season 13 a memorable one despite the cast cut-offs.

    In March 2023, Kyle stated in an interview with ET, explaining that “there are going to be some faces missing this season,” but Season 13 won’t be any less drama or entertainment. She added:

    “It’s always interesting to see how the dynamics shift, as other people come and go, and it makes for an interesting show. And it’s interesting to watch now how people who didn’t get along, do, or the ones that did, now don’t. Just changes everything.”

    Real Housewives of Beverly Hills Season 13 release date

    As for when exactly Real Housewives will return to the Bravo screen, Garcelle Beauvais confirmed in March 2023 that filming for Season 13 had already begun in February 2023. She added that fans could expect the “old school Beverly Hills in terms of glamour and fun” in the upcoming season to raise excitement.

    As of June 2023, the filming for The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills Season 13 has officially wrapped up, and viewers may anticipate getting their first peek at the new season in the following months. Teasing the release date of RHOBH Season 13, Beauvais spoke at the Banff World Media Festival in June 2023:

    “I’m hearing November is our release date. It’s back to fun and glamour and friendships. It’s not as toxic as it was. I think the last season was pretty hardcore on everybody.”

    So, fans can expect the season to hit the screens this November, though no official word on the release date has been made public so far. The premiere might occur close to BravoCon 2023, which will be held November 3-5 in Las Vegas — if the rumors of a November release date are accurate.

    The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills Season 13 will air on Bravo, with new episodes streaming the following day on Peacock. You can catch up on all previous seasons on Peacock right now, and UK viewers can catch up on the past seasons on Hayu.
